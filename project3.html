Eco Life App

EcoLife is an innovative app designed to promote recycling through social media engagement and gamification

The project objective is to encourage and educate users on the importance of recycling while fostering sustainable practices through an engaging, easy-touse app. The app is designed for users of all ages, from kids to adults, incorporating aspects of social media and gaming for maximum impact.
